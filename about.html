Who We Are

The VECTOR Research Lab‚Äîwhere Vision, Embeddings, Cognitive science, Text processing, Optimization, and Reasoning converge‚Äîinvestigates how intelligent systems learn from diverse modalities to better understand and support human experience. We develop resource-efficient, ethically aligned AI methods that scale across tasks and domains. Our work spans foundational modelling, applied machine learning, and cross-disciplinary computational research.

Our Approach

Through our six core pillars (Vision, Embeddings, Cognitive, Text, Optimization, Reasoning), we combine insights from cognitive science, linguistics, computer vision, and symbolic reasoning to create methods that are robust, interpretable, and beneficial for society. We are committed to open research, reproducibility, and developing technologies that expand access to high-quality AI capabilities worldwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance the science and engineering of multimodal, human-centred, and knowledge-guided AI. We aim to design machine learning systems that adapt to real-world complexity, reason over heterogeneous information, and operate effectively even under low-resource constraints. By integrating insights from cognitive science, linguistics, computer vision, and symbolic reasoning, we create methods that are robust, interpretable, and beneficial for society.
