Collaboration Opportunities

We co-design studies, shared datasets, and applied pilots with universities, civic groups, and industry partners who align with our mission.

Cross-Disciplinary Research

Partner with cognitive science, linguistics, HCI, CV, robotics, and educational technology teams. We welcome joint grant proposals and sabbatical exchanges.

  • Shared PhD supervision
  • Joint dataset releases
  • Co-authored position papers

Community & Social Impact Pilots

Deploy resource-efficient AI for public-good settings (education, accessibility, linguistic diversity). We prioritise participatory design.

  • Non-profit data collaborations
