Training & Development for Researchers

Building sustainable publication pipelines and research profiles for the next generation

Our Philosophy

We don't just help you publish one paper—we train you to build sustainable publication pipelines that serve your entire career. Our approach focuses on capacity building, skill development, and creating systems that support long-term research productivity.

Our Commitment

As active researchers in multimodal AI who navigate publication processes ourselves, we understand the challenges you face. We share our knowledge, strategies, and systems to help you develop your own research communication capabilities.

Cohort-Based Learning

Why Cohorts?

Research can be isolating. Learning with peers who share your challenges creates accountability, diverse perspectives, and lasting professional networks.

Cohort benefits:

  • Peer learning from researchers at similar career stages
  • Accountability through group progress tracking
  • Diverse perspectives on research and publication strategies
  • Professional network building with future collaborators
  • Shared resources and knowledge exchange
  • Motivation and support through challenges

Current Cohort Programs

Publication Pipeline Cohort

Small groups (8-12 researchers) working together over 8-12 weeks to develop sustainable publication systems, review each other's work, and build lasting publication habits.

Research Portfolio Development

Professional Research Websites

Build your digital research presence with a professional portfolio website that showcases your work to the international academic community.

What We Help You Create

Essential Components:

  • Professional homepage with research overview
  • Publications page with organized research output
  • Project portfolio with descriptions and visuals
  • About/CV page with academic background
  • Contact and collaboration information

Why This Matters:

A professional research website serves as your permanent academic home, independent of institution affiliations. It's the first place potential collaborators, funders, and students look to understand your work.

Our Approach:

We don't just build the website—we train you to maintain and update it yourself. You'll understand the structure, know how to add publications, update projects, and evolve your site as your research grows.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from university writing centers?

We combine domain expertise in AI/ML research with publication experience in top venues. We understand your technical content and the specific expectations of your target journals/conferences.

Do I need to be in a specific field?

While our research is in multimodal AI, our training principles apply broadly. We work with researchers across computational fields and adjacent disciplines.

What's the time commitment?

Varies by program. Individual workshops are 2-3 hours. Multi-week programs typically require 3-5 hours per week. We design programs to fit researcher schedules.

Can I join programs individually or do I need a group?

Both. We offer individual enrollment in cohort programs and also work with existing research groups or institutional arrangements.

Do you provide ongoing support after training?

Many programs include follow-up support periods. We also offer alumni communities for continued peer connection.

Is training available remotely?

Yes, most programs are available online with live interactive sessions. We also offer in-person options for institutional programs.

Do you work with non-native English speakers?

Yes, we have specific experience training international researchers and addressing language-specific challenges in academic writing.
