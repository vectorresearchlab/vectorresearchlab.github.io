Human-Centred AI

Designing intelligent systems grounded in human needs, cognition, behaviour, and societal context. We build AI that adapts to real-world complexity and human experience, integrating insights from cognitive science and human-computer interaction.

Example Focus Areas

  • Cognitive modelling and mental state inference
  • Adaptive interfaces and personalisation
  • Human-AI collaboration systems
üîÑ

Multimodal & Behavioural Learning

Building models that learn from and reason across languages, vision, signals, structured knowledge, and human behaviour. We develop architectures that integrate heterogeneous data sources for comprehensive understanding.

Example Focus Areas

  • Vision-language models and cross-modal reasoning
  • Behavioural pattern recognition
  • Multi-sensor fusion architectures
üîó

Symbolic + Neural Integration

Combining symbolic reasoning with neural architectures to create models that explain, justify, and reveal underlying patterns. We bridge the gap between connectionist and symbolic AI paradigms.

Example Focus Areas

  • Neural-symbolic reasoning frameworks
  • Knowledge graph integration
  • Interpretable deep learning
‚ö°

Scalable & Low-Resource AI

Developing methods that perform reliably in low-resource environments, generalise across domains, and remain computationally affordable. We focus on efficiency, transfer learning, and few-shot capabilities.

Example Focus Areas

  • Parameter-efficient fine-tuning
  • Cross-lingual transfer learning
  • Edge AI and mobile deployment
